Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This policy covers the April Hour Publisher app, which connects our Instagram business account (@aprilhouraiagents) to our own tools.

Who we are

April Hour · AI Agents builds done-for-you AI systems for small businesses. It operates under Days of April (Private) Limited, Sri Lanka. You can reach us any time at aprilhouraiagents@gmail.com.

What this app does

The app lets our own systems publish our approved Instagram posts, reply to comments on our posts, and answer direct messages sent to our account. It works only on our account. It does not read or touch anyone else's account.

What information it handles

  • Messages and comments you send us. If you comment on our posts or message our account, the app reads that text and your username so it can reply.
  • Enquiry records. If you ask about our services, we keep your username and your question in our private client records so a real person can follow up.

That is the full list. The app collects nothing else: no contact lists, no browsing data, no information about your account beyond the message you chose to send us.

What we never do

  • We never sell your information, to anyone.
  • We never share it with third parties for marketing or advertising.
  • We never message you first. The app only replies when you comment or write to us.

How long we keep things

Technical delivery records are deleted automatically within 24 hours. Enquiry records stay in our private client list until the enquiry is closed, or until you ask us to remove them.

Your choices

Want your enquiry record deleted, or a copy of what we hold about you? Email aprilhouraiagents@gmail.com or send us a DM. We do it by hand and confirm when it is done.
